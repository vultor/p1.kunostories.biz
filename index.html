Shawn Roe

Bio

Born and raised in Virginia, Shawn Graduated from Mr. Jefferson's University with a BA in Psychology, and a minor in Astronomy... yes, Astronomy.

World traveler and story-teller who began his adventures as an English teacher in South Korea, Shawn has since been lucky enough to climb the Great Wall in China, surf in 5 different countries including Indonesia and Costa Rica, hike Mt. Fuji, drive the length of New Zealand, and width of the United States (3 times), live a mile from the Pacific Ocean in Southern California, volunteer as a plumber in Peru, hike to Machu Pichuu, and be blessed with loving, supportive, and inspiring friends and family along the way.

His hobbies include hiking, biking, playing baseball, surfing, snowboarding, creating websites, reading, and traveling.
